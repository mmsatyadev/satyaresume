WEBSITE FOE SOFT DRINKS


Front End : HTML,CSS

ABSTRACT


    Marketing is one of the most fundamental things in a business, as it helps obtain people's attention. Printing postcard to send out even to just a local audience can be expensive, there is no guarantee that the recipient is interested in your business or the product/service you offer. Nowadays digital marketing has a wider scope, it will help businesses acquire a global audience's attention. Digital marketing can save a lot of money compared to printing individual postcards and paying for postage on each. Instead of marketing through costly banners and printed cards we have done a Digital Marketing project by using Social meadia, Email marketing website (Mailchimp), online marketing (using HTML&CSS) and also by google maps. Comparing to traditional marketing it is a very less time consuming process. And also it is an effective way to take our products directly to the customers.
